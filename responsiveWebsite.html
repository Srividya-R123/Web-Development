Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food dish originating from Mumbai. It consists of a spicy and flavorful vegetable curry, known as "bhaji," served with buttered and toasted pav buns. It is often garnished with chopped onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lemon for added freshness.

Pani Puri

Pani Puri is a popular Indian street food delight. It consists of hollow, crispy puris filled with a spicy and tangy tamarind-flavored water, chickpeas and potatoes. This mouthwatering snack offers a burst of refreshing flavors, making it a favorite among food enthusiasts across the country.

Poli

Poli is a popular Indian sweet delicacy made during festivals and special occasions. It consists of thin, soft flatbreads filled with a sweet mixture of jaggery and lentils or coconut. This delightful treat is often enjoyed with a touch of ghee or as a delicious accompaniment to a festive meal.

Samosa

Samosa is a popular Indian snack that is loved for its crispy and flaky pastry filled with a savory mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes lentils. This triangular-shaped delight is deep-fried to golden perfection, making it a delightful treat for any occasion. Samosas are commonly served with chutneys or sauces, adding an extra burst of flavor to this mouthwatering snack.
